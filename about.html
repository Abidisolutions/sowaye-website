Our Purpose

We built the platform we
always wished existed

Sowaye was born out of frustration with fragmented HR tools, missed project deadlines, and endless spreadsheets. We set out to build one unified platform that gives every role exactly what they need.

Our Story

From Internal Tool to
Global Solution

Sowaye started as ABIDI Pro, an internal HRM and project management system built by Abidi Solutions to manage their own workforce. After solving our own operational challenges, we rebuilt it as a professional product for the world.

Internal system pedigree

  • → Employee onboarding & RBAC
  • → Project timelines & budgets
  • → Task dependencies & tracking
  • → Time logging & timesheets

  • → Multi-company management
  • → Deep Azure ecosystem integration
